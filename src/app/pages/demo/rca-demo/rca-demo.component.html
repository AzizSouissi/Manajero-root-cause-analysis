Limitations and Acceptations



Advantages Limitations
Solves recurring issues effectively. Requires skilled facilitators for effective sessions.
Improves decision-making based on data-driven insights. Depends heavily on thorough data collection and analysis.
Facilitates resolution of complex problems. Can be time-consuming during data collection and analysis.
Encourages cross-functional collaboration. Requires regular data updates to remain effective.
Improves customer satisfaction by identifying and eliminating recurring issues. May not capture all external factors influencing a problem.

RCA-Tutoriel

Why

Understanding the root causes allows organizations to implement lasting solutions, reducing the likelihood of recurring issues and improving overall efficiency. RCA enables a proactive approach to problem-solving by focusing on underlying factors rather than just addressing symptoms.
